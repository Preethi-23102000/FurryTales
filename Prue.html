Meet Prue

This beautiful, little 5 month old Tabby is sure to steal your heart!

Prue is super friendly and loves to be held and kissed and will chill on your lap waiting patiently for pets and belly rubs! She is an observer and loves a nice window seat view.

Prue enjoys playing with jingle balls and her catnip kicker and will even choose playing over treats if in a playful mood!

Prue has spent time with other cats and dogs and although we are still in the introduction stages, she appears unphased by their presence (she even lets our small dog lick her). We believe she would be able make new friends! Prue has been vaccinated and is awaiting her spay surgery. She is negative for FIV/FeLV. Prue would be an easy, adorable addition to any household that would give her lots of love and attention!
