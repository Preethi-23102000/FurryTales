Meet Bri

One year old Bri is a very special pup full of character. At her foster home, she lives with a 6 year old lab mix and a 3 year old toddler. She gets along well with other dogs and is very gentle with kids.

Unlike other dogs who love playing with balls and sticks, Bri enjoys playing with soft toys. Although it could be hard to believe she doesn’t destroy her toys. One of her favorite things is chasing squirrels in the backyard and sitting patiently in the sun waiting for them to show up. Bri is so expressive, she loves everyone, likes to stay close and loves to snuggle./p>

Bri knows how to sit and has a very good recall. She is potty trained and crate trained. At night, her crate and a comfy blanket is all she needs. ????When it’s bedtime, Bri gets into her crate and curls up in her blanket on her own, ready to sleep through the night. She loves going on car rides to explore hiking trails and also loves going for walks. Bri is such a fun dog, full of energy. She would be a lovely addition to any family.

Bri is about 1 year old, healthy, fully vetted, on regular monthly flea and heartworm prevention as well as microchipped. If you would like additional information on this sweet girl or would like a meet and greet contact us through the website or email.
